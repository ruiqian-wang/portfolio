Event Poster

This is a poster of 2024 Water Lantern Festival in Sacramento. I aimed to present a enchanting atmosphere of the event. By combining three distinct images, I created a scene that evokes the ambiance of the festival, with lanterns gently floating on water against a picturesque backdrop. To enhance the cohesion and warmth of the imagery, I applied a gradient map to harmonize the colors and imbue the scene with a sense of familial warmth.
