Problem Statement

Due to stigma, lack of support, and inflexible school policies, pregnant teens and young mothers are often forced to leave school, facing lifelong challenges that hinder their personal development and the well-being of their children while perpetuating cycles of poverty and social inequality.

How Might We?

Create a platform that provides personalized educational and support solutions for pregnant teens and young mothers so that they can receive customized assistance and resources designed to their unique needs, enabling them to continue their education and improve their long-term prospects?

Persona

About

  • Age: 18
  • Location: Suburban area
  • Education: High school graduate
  • Socio-Economic Status: Low to moderate income

Background

Ava is a high school graduate who had her baby shortly after graduating. She has been unable to pursue higher education due to financial constraints and lack of local educational opportunities. She is considering returning to school but faces significant barriers due to her current circumstances.

Goals

  • Find affordable and flexible educational opportunities.
  • Improve her financial situation and gain employment skills.
  • Provide a stable environment for her child.

Pain Points

  • Lack of local educational institutions.
  • Social stigma and isolation from peers who have not experienced similar challenges.

Solution

Holistic Support

Offer both practical and emotional support. From career resources to mental health tracking and peer support, the app will integrate essential features that cater to the multi-faceted needs of young mothers.

Personalized Experience

The app will provide tailored experiences, adapting to each user's unique situation, whether it's through learning suggestions, parenting tips, or career development resources, ensuring the content feels relevant and valuable.

Information Access

Critical information, such as access to crisis support, will be easy to find, reducing the time and effort young mothers need to spend navigating complex systems.

Encouraging Growth

Empower young mothers to pursue education and career development by making resources easily accessible, offering guidance through every step.

Lessons & Outcomes

Balancing Functionality and Simplicity

One of the major challenges was integrating the broad range of features, from education to community support, without overwhelming users. I had to make conscious decisions on what features should take priority and how they should be presented. Larger fonts help ensure key information stands out immediately, making it easier for users to engage with crucial elements in a dense design.

Iterative Process and Flexibility

I realized the importance of keeping the design process flexible. Certain assumptions I had about the app's use and structure didn't hold up during early testing, and I had to redesign entire sections based on user real using cases. This iterative process ultimately strengthened the app's core offering.
